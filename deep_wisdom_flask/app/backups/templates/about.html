About

In short, DeepWisdom is a platform that augments your understanding of the bible. This is done by finding unintuitive relationships within and between interrelated content. Hence, DeepWisdom is a platform that blends the greatest wisdom in antiquity with state of the art Artificial Intelligence. Converting knowledge into understanding is one of the most profound and challenging tasks a human can face. This is where we come in, by integrating classical analytics techniques with state of the art Artificial Intelligence we are able to take your seeking and understanding of the bible to an entirely new level. For the first time in history, empowered by DeepWisdom, we are able to explore and discover novel, unintuitive, and purhaps otherwise, undiscovered relationships from deep within scripture.

Objective

The goal of DeepWisdom is two-fold. First, offer a robust platform that yields truly unique and novel insights into scripture that would have otherwise gone undiscovered or unnoticed. Second, offer a globally accessible platform that effectively equipts all in their personal pursuit of wisdom with true value and insights.
