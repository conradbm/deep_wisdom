About

In short, DeepWisdom is a platform that augments your understanding of the bible. The conversion of knowledge to understanding has perplexed biblical scholars, philosophers, mathematicians, and psychologists for centuries. Here at DeepWisdom, we seek to bridge this gap. From modern Deep Learning synthesized with ancient wisdom found in scripture, we integrate analytics to find deep connections for you.

Objective

The goal of DeepWisdom is two-fold. First, to truly connect the dots for you within scripture; from characters, to parables and stories, to psalms, proverbs and statements. Second, offer a globally accessible platform that effectively equipts all in their personal pursuit of wisdom.
